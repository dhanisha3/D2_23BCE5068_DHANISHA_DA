Delivery Tips & Best Practices

Security

  • Always verify QR code authenticity
  • Check delivery ID matches package label
  • Ensure locker is properly locked after deposit
  • Report any suspicious activity immediately

Efficiency

  • Pre-scan QR codes before arriving
  • Choose lockers close to your route
  • Handle fragile items with extra care
  • Complete deposits during off-peak hours

Quality

  • Protect packages from weather
  • Position items to prevent damage
  • Take photos for delivery confirmation
  • Update delivery status promptly
